FAQs



How can we be able to contact the artist?

Every portfolio includes the contact details of each artist such as their emails, business numbers, work addresses etc.

How will we know the commission rates of the works?

The commission rates can be found by directly contacting the artists about specific works.

Are the artworks for sale?

Artworks that are for sale and not for sale are labeled as is. The artwork may be up for sale depending on the artists’ decision.

Artisan is defined as someone skilled in applied art. This platform allows other artists to have their created works shown all on one page. Having their artworks included in this platform allows others to view their works and learn more about them as well. Artisan aims to create an aesthetic wherein people can look into the creativity and design of others.
